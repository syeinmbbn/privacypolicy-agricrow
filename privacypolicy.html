Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

Welcome to the AgriCrow! Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our system, mobile application, and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: User-provided details such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Device Information: Data about the device used to access the Scarecrow System
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our application.
  • Real-Time Data: Video or image feeds captured by the ESP32-CAM modules for bird detection.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain the Scarecrow System.
  • Provide real-time monitoring and bird detection alerts.
  • Notify users of system activities through SMS or app notifications.
  • Enhance system features and user experience.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. However, we may share your data to comply with legal obligations or with service providers like Firebase.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information as long as your account remains active. Video and image feeds are deleted after real-time processing.

5. Your Privacy Rights

  • Access and Update: Review and update your personal information in the app.
  • Account Deletion: Request account deletion by following the steps on our Delete Account page.

6. Data Security

We use encryption and secure storage practices to protect your data and regularly update our systems to patch vulnerabilities.
